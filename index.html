Global climate crisis

Our planet is heating faster than at any time in modern history.

Climate change is no longer a distant forecast. It is a present reality, reshaping heatwaves, rainfall, oceans, and lives in every region of the world. This site advocates for responsible, ethical climate action rooted in science and justice.

Presented by Mayur J

2024 global temperature
≈ 1.46 °C above 1850–1900

2024 was the warmest year on record, with global surface temperatures about 1.46 °C warmer than the pre‑industrial average (NOAA, NASA).

Decade of extremes
10 / 10 hottest years

The ten warmest years since 1850 have all occurred in the past decade, driven mainly by human greenhouse‑gas emissions.

Why this matters now

Rising temperatures are amplifying extreme heat, flooding, wildfires, and sea‑level rise. The heaviest burdens fall on communities that contributed the least to emissions, raising profound ethical questions about equity, responsibility, and intergenerational justice.
